## Example Google Form Layout

### Team Availability & Scheduling Submission Form

Use this form to track:
- Remote work (WFH)
- Vacation time
- Out-of-office availability
- Other scheduling absences

The purpose of this workflow is to maintain team scheduling visibility and reduce conflicts through automated calendar synchronization.

---

### Example Fields

#### Team Member
- Team Member A
- Team Member B
- Team Member C
- Team Member D

---

#### Availability Type
- Vacation
- Work From Home (WFH)
- Out of Office / Available
- Other

---

Date selector input

#### Start Time
(Optional for single-day events)

Date selector input

#### End Time
(Optional for single-day events)

---

#### Additional Notes
Optional comments or scheduling notes

---

## Automated Workflow

Google Form → Google Sheets → Apps Script → Google Calendar → Email Summary Reporting

---

## Features

- Automatic Google Calendar event generation
- Team scheduling synchronization
- Shared availability tracking
- Automated weekly summary emails
- Input validation and formatting
- Support for timed and multi-day events
